WAR!

The card game.

WAR!

GAME SETUP:
A deck of cards is shuffled and split between the player and the computer, face down.

GAME PLAY:
When DEAL is clicked, the top card of each player's deck is flipped over and compared. The player with the highest card wins both cards. In the event of a tie, there will be WAR!
WAR:
Three cards will be drawn from each deck (if available - only as many will be drawn so that the player has one card left to flip) and the fourth (or last) card from each will be compared. The player with the highest card wins all "war" cards.

GAME END:
The game ends when one player holds all of the cards.
